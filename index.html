BINGO!

Welcome to Bingo! Please select a difficulty and then generate numbers OR generate numbers without a card at all! Your selected difficulty will determine how large your card number possibilities can be (If you don't select, you'll be defaulted to easy. ! Don't worry, repeats ARE allowed ;)! Everytime you generate a number, your 'counter' will go up! When you press play again, the game will reset to that certain difficulty. When you see the number you generated on your board, click the number to mark it! If you marked by accident, click again to unmark. Try to win BINGO (five diagonals or five in any direction) with the lowest counter! Good luck!
